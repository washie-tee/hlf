About Us

Learn, Play & Grow at Happy Little Feet

We provide a warm, safe, and stimulating learning environment where children build confidence, creativity, and curiosity through play-based learning. Our caring teachers guide each child’s unique journey.

  • Safe, nurturing environment
  • Qualified, caring teachers
  • Play-based, holistic learning
  • Strong family partnerships

Programs & Activities

Our holistic program develops creativity, curiosity, confidence, and physical well-being.

Learn Through Play

Play-based learning that builds problem-solving and social skills.

Art & Craft

Creative projects to explore colors, textures, and imagination.

Language (incl. French)

Early literacy and communication with exposure to French.

Robotics

Hands-on STEM activities introducing coding and robotics.

Music

Rhythm, singing, and instruments to nurture expression.

Swimming

Water confidence and safety in a fun, supervised setting.

Educational Tours

Real-world learning through curated trips and experiences.

Sports

Active play and teamwork to build strength and coordination.

School Facilities

Our school offers safe, well-maintained spaces that support learning, play, and healthy growth. From classrooms to outdoor areas, every facility is designed to nurture curiosity and confidence.

School Bus

Reliable transport with trained drivers and seat-belted vehicles for safe pick-up and drop-off.

Playground

Large, supervised play areas with safe equipment that encourage active play and teamwork.

Healthy Canteen

Nutritious, child-friendly meals prepared hygienically, with options for allergies and preferences.

Positive Learning

Bright, well-equipped classrooms where teachers foster confidence, curiosity, and respect.

School Classes

Explore our Nursery, ECD, and Primary classes designed to build strong foundations in literacy, numeracy, creativity, and social skills. Small groups and caring teachers ensure every child is supported at each stage.

Admissions

We welcome applications year‑round. Follow the steps below to apply and join our caring learning community.

Application Steps

  1. 1
    Visit & Tour

    Schedule a school tour to see our facilities and meet our teachers.

  2. 2
    Submit Application

    Provide the completed form and required documents listed below.

  3. 3
    Assessment & Placement

    We review your application and advise on the appropriate class level.

  4. 4
    Enrollment Confirmation

    Complete enrollment by paying fees and confirming your start date.

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Child’s birth certificate (copy)
  • Recent passport‑size photo
  • Immunization/medical record (if available)
  • Previous school report (for transfers)

Frequently Asked Questions

What ages do you accept?

We enroll children from nursery through primary levels. Placement is based on age and readiness.

When can my child start?

We accept applications year‑round. Start dates may align with term calendars or rolling admissions.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. Please speak with our admissions team to discuss available options.
